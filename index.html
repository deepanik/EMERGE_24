Join our glorious event to celebrate entrepreneurship and innovation.

Centre for Entrepreneurship Education & Development, Chitkara University, Himachal Pradesh announces our annual Innovation and Entrepreneurship Fest "EMERGE'24": It’s a Congregation of Start-up Ecosystem Builder, EMERGE’24 is the flagship event conducted by CEED, Chitkara University with an objective to celebrate the entrepreneurial zeal of our nation and meet entrepreneurs, inventors, innovators, investors, mentors, industry captains and youth under one common platform.
